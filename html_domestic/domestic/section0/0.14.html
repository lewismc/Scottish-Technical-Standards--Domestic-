14
domestic | general | clearing of footpaths | 2005
regulation
mandatory
Where any work is being carried out on a building site or building ,
any neighbouring footpath (including any footpath provided so as to
form part of the protective works ) shall be regularly cleaned and kept
free of building debris and related materials by the person carrying
out the work , to the satisfaction of the local authority.
0.14.1 Explanation
Regulation 14 requires the keeping free from mud or dust footpaths adjacent
to building sites .
