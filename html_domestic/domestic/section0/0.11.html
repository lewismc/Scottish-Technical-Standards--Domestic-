Every service, fitting or piece of equipment provided so as to serve a
purpose of these regulations shall be so provided in such a way as to
further those purposes.
0.11.1 Explanation
Regulation 11 requires that every service, fitting or piece of equipment
provided so as to serve a purpose of the regulations should be designed,
installed, and commissioned in such a way as to fulfill those purposes.
